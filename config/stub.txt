gameloop
read role:int
loop 7 read boardRow:word(7)
write 3 1 3 0

STATEMENT
An ancient Celtic strategy game. A small group of defenders must help their king escape, while a large force of attackers tries to capture him.

INPUT
role: 0 or 1
boardRow: A 7-character string representing a row of the board. '.' is empty, 'A' is attacker, 'D' is defender, 'K' is the king.

OUTPUT
3 1 3 0
